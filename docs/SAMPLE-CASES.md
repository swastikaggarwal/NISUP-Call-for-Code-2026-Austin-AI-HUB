# 🧪 Sample Cases — test NISUP in 5 minutes

Copy-paste these scripts into the **[live app](https://nisup-call-for-code-2026-austin-ai.vercel.app)** to experience every feature exactly as shown in the [demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oMYR1PRZMWw). You can **type or speak** every message (Chrome/Edge, allow the microphone to talk).

> Start: open the live app → **Begin**. To reset between cases, click the **NISUP logo** (top-left).

---

## Case 1 · 🔴 Amit — forced labour in Dubai (red flags + nexus + filing)

The full victim journey: red-flag detection → the 86% nexus match → a filed, trackable case.

**Message 1** — paste and send:

```
Hello, I need to talk to someone. My name is Amit. I came from India to Dubai three months ago for a hotel cleaning job. A man called Ravi Kumar arranged everything and promised me 2000 dirhams every month.
```

**Message 2** — after NISUP replies, paste and send:

```
When I arrived, his agent took my passport at the airport and said I owe 9000 dirhams for the visa and flight. Now they make me work 14 hours every day and pay me nothing. We are 8 people sleeping in one room and the door is locked from outside at night. Ravi's phone number is +971 50 123 4567.
```

⏳ *Wait ~10 seconds after the reply. You should see:*
- 🚩 the **red-flag card** — passport confiscation, debt bondage, confinement, named plainly
- 🕸️ the **nexus card** — *"I've seen a related case — 86% match"*: Ravi Kumar was already reported by a survivor weeks earlier (seeded demo case `NIS-RAVI1`)

**Message 3** — the ask:

```
I am afraid, but I do not want this to happen to anyone else. Can you please help me out and file this case for me?
```

→ Confirm filing → review the **auto-drafted complaint** (type, location, urgency, people, evidence, routing) → **Send this report** → you receive a **Case ID** → open **Track this case** to see the live timeline and the handling authority's contacts.

## Case 2 · 👁️ Witness — a child working in a hotel (anonymous, with photo)

Shows zero-friction third-party reporting. Choose **"I witnessed something"** on the welcome cards (or just say it), then paste:

```
I saw a kid working in a nearby hotel. He is just 7 years old, and also yesterday I saw him on Dwarka Delhi Road. I am sharing the location and his photo I clicked.
```

Optionally attach any photo and location → keep **Stay anonymous** ON → **Publish my story**.

→ Published in seconds, routed to the child-protection authority, with a reference ID — the witness never reveals their identity.

---

## Case 3 · 🕊️ Survivor — sharing a story to protect others

Choose **"I'm a survivor"** (or open the share card) and paste:

```
Hi all, my name is Shanaya. I am sharing a case with you that I was trapped in Dubai in 2025. They took my passport, and those people are really dangerous — they take us in the name of employment.
```

→ Publish (anonymously if preferred). The story becomes a **public case article** that warns others — and feeds the nexus engine so future reports naming the same people or places get matched.

---

## Case 4 · 🟢 Green flag — a doubt, NOT a case

Shows that NISUP never manufactures cases. Start fresh and paste:

```
An agency offered my cousin a job abroad but asks a 2000 rupee registration fee first. Is that normal?
```

→ NISUP gives a clear answer and practical checkpoints (verify the licence, never surrender original documents, written contract first) and closes warmly. **No case is filed, no pressure.** A doubt gets a calm answer; a pattern gets a case — NISUP knows the difference.

## Case 5 · 🏛️ The Authority Console

Open **[/dashboard/overview](https://nisup-call-for-code-2026-austin-ai.vercel.app/dashboard/overview)** — demo mode, any email/password signs in.

What to look at:
1. **KPI tiles + urgency mix** — the live caseload at a glance
2. **🕸️ Pattern Alerts** — scored network links (*"82% similar"*) with the exact shared signals: same phone, same recruiter, same district. If you filed **Case 1**, Amit's report now strengthens the Ravi Kumar network.
3. **🚩 Incoming cases** — red-flagged cases float to the top; open one to see the AI-written brief, risk indicators, linked cases, and the suggested action
4. **Map + geographic hotspots** — where reports concentrate
5. Change a case's status to **In Review** → the reporter's **tracking page** updates within seconds — transparency both ways.
